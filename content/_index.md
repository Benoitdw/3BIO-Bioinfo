The 3BIO-BioInfo laboratory is part of the **ULB-VUB (IB)² Interuniversity Institute of Bioinformatics in Brussels**. We develop cutting-edge bioinformatic tools and computational approaches for biology, biotechnology and medicine research.

Our research emphasizes strong connections with experimental labs from both academia and industry, bridging the gap between theoretical predictions and practical applications.
