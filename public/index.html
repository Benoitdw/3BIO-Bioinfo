Welcome to 3BIO-BioInfo

The 3BIO-BioInfo laboratory is part of the ULB-VUB (IB)² Interuniversity Institute of Bioinformatics in Brussels. We develop cutting-edge bioinformatic tools and computational approaches for biology, biotechnology and medicine research.

Our research emphasizes strong connections with experimental labs from both academia and industry, bridging the gap between theoretical predictions and practical applications.

Research Focus

Our lab develops bioinformatic tools using artificial intelligence, machine learning, data analysis, statistical physics, stochastic modelling and quantum chemistry for applications in biology, biotechnology and medicine.

Key Research Areas

  • Predicting protein mutations' effects on biophysical properties (stability, solubility, binding affinity)
  • DNA ionization potential prediction
  • Epitope prediction for vaccine and antibody design
  • Stochastic modeling of biological systems
