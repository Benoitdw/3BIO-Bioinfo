3BIO-BioInfo

We develop bioinformatic tools using artificial intelligence, machine learning, data analysis, statistical physics, stochastic modelling and quantum chemistry for applications in biology, biotechnology and medicine.

About Us

We focus on developing bioinformatics tools and computational approaches to address key challenges in biology, biotechnology, and medicine. Integrating balanced theoretical foundations with fundamental biological principles, we strive to translate complex data into meaningful scientific insights.
