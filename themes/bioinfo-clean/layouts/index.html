Research Focus

Our lab develops bioinformatic tools using artificial intelligence, machine learning, data analysis, statistical physics, stochastic modelling and quantum chemistry for applications in biology, biotechnology and medicine.

Key Research Areas

  • Predicting protein mutations' effects on biophysical properties (stability, solubility, binding affinity)
  • DNA ionization potential prediction
  • Epitope prediction for vaccine and antibody design
  • Stochastic modeling of biological systems
